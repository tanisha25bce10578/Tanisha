## Stay Safe online- Checklist:
1. Don't post too much personal information on social media, like your phone number, address, college ID, travel plans, and so on.
   
2. Make your password very strong until it is hard to guess for everyone.
   
3. Deactivate and delete unused "ghost" accounts to minimize your searchable digital footprint.
   
4. Don't ever give out your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank information to anyone, even if they say they work for the bank or support team.
   
5. Deny "Always On" location access for apps that don't need it to save battery and privacy.
   
6. To avoid fraud, make sure to check UPI IDs and QR codes carefully before paying.
   
7. Don't use public wifi to log into your bank or school accounts. Instead, use your phone's data.
   
8. For your email, social media, and school accounts, use strong, unique passwords and turn on two-factor authentication.
   
9. If you see any cyber fraud, call the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or the Cyber Helpline at 1930 right away.
    
10. Don't click on links in emails, WhatsApp messages, or SMS that look like they might be scams for prizes, bank alerts, or job offers.
