## *To help you build a professional and safe presence , here is detailed Do's and Don'ts checklist for checklist.*

# 5 DO'S:
1. Do share what you love
   
2. Always verify information before sharing and believing in them.
   
3. Take a look at your "Privacy" menu.
   
4. Treat people the same way you would talk to them in person.
   
5. Use social media for learning,networking and all the activities which can be beneficial for you.

# 5 DONT'S:
1. Don't argue with "Trolls".
   
2. Don’t share personal information and contacts.
   
3. Don't compare yourself.
   
4. Don’t post and promote offensive content.
   
5. Never cyberbully or troll others on social media.
